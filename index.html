    Lifetime support means we're available for bug fixes, demo prep, and viva questions — even after delivery. If something breaks before your demo day, we fix it. If your examiner requests a change during the presentation, we handle it. No extra charges.

    We support CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, and MBA streams. Our stack covers software, embedded systems, IoT, data analysis, AI/ML, and web development — so we can find the right approach for your branch and requirements.
