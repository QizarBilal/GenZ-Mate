    Yes, unconditionally. All code, assets, and documentation are transferred to you upon project completion. No licensing restrictions, no subscriptions, no strings attached.

    Documentation is included in Standard and Complete packages. This covers your full project report, abstract, literature survey, system design, and results sections — formatted to your college's submission standard.

    Deployment is included in the Complete package. We host your application on a cloud provider, configure the domain, set up SSL, and hand you a live URL — not a localhost demo.

    Lifetime maintenance is included in the Complete package. If something breaks before your viva, we fix it. If your examiner asks for a change during the demo, we handle it.

    Every project is built to your college's specific requirements — from documentation format to technology stack preferences. We ask for your department guidelines at the start and follow them exactly.
